FAQ

Questions before you start?

How does 1stStep.ai tailor my resume?

It compares your resume to the job description, identifies relevant language and gaps, then rewrites your resume around the role while preserving your actual experience.

Do I need to tailor my resume for every job?

Yes, if you want the strongest odds. Many screening systems and recruiters look for role-specific alignment, not just general experience.

Will it make things up?

No. The product is designed to reframe and prioritize your real experience, not invent skills, companies, titles, or credentials.

Can I cancel Job Hunt Pass?

Yes. Job Hunt Pass is monthly and built for the period when you are actively applying.

Is the resume ATS-friendly?

Yes. The output uses clear, recruiter-readable wording and avoids design choices that commonly break applicant tracking systems.
