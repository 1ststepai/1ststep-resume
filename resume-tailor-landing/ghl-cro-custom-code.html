FAQ

Questions before you start?

Is 1stStep.ai just another AI resume writer?

No. It is built around the application workflow: upload your resume, paste a job description, get a match score, tailor the resume, create a cover letter, and save the version you used.

Will it make things up?

The goal is to reframe your real experience around the target role, not invent companies, titles, credentials, or skills you do not have.

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. The free account includes 3 tailored resumes so you can test the workflow before upgrading.

Is my resume information private?

Your resume is treated as private application data. The promise is simple: we do not sell your resume information.
