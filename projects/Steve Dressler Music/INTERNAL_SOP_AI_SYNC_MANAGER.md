# 🛠 Internal SOP: AI Sync Asset Management

**Authorized Agents:** Claude / Antigravity **Context:** Management of the Steve Dressler 68-track catalog. **Primary Objective:** Maintain the Sync CRM, audit assets, and execute "Sniper" outreach.

## 1. Required AI Skillsets & Modes

When executing this SOP, the AI should rotate between these three "Mindsets":

1. **The Librarian (Technical):** Focuses on ISRC codes, file naming conventions, and ID3 metadata accuracy.
    
2. **The Music Supervisor (Curatorial):** Matches the "vibe" of a track to specific TV shows or cinematic scenes.
    
3. **The Agent (Persuasive):** Writes high-conversion, low-friction emails to industry decision-makers.
    

## 2. Inclusive Initialization Prompt

_Paste this prompt first whenever starting a new session with Claude/Antigravity regarding this client:_

> "Act as a Senior Sync Licensing Agent. Your knowledge base is the Steve Dressler Music Catalog (68 tracks). You have access to the 'Sync CRM Index' in my Obsidian vault. Your goal is to ensure 100% asset readiness and execute targeted pitching.
> 
> **Your Constraints:**
> 
> - Everything is 'One-Stop' (100% Master/100% Publishing).
>     
> - Tone must be professional, brief, and industry-literate.
>     
> - Always cross-reference SEO keywords to generate 'Mood' metadata."
>     

## 3. Data Processing & Auditing Prompts

**Skill: Technical Audit**

> "Analyze the attached Track Notes for [Track Name]. Compare the 'SEO Description' to the 'Sync Metadata' properties.
> 
> 1. Suggest 5 'Mood' tags based on the description.
>     
> 2. Check the 'Assets Checklist'—if 'Instrumental' or '30s Sting' is unchecked, create a priority task for the engineer."
>     

**Skill: Mood Mapping**

> "I have a lead for a show similar to 'Succession' that needs gritty, tension-filled solo guitar. Query the '01 Catalog' folder and identify the top 3 tracks that fit this sonic profile. Explain _why_ they fit based on their metadata."

## 4. The "Sniper" Outreach Template (Inclusive Prompt)

_Use this when you want the AI to draft an email:_

> "Draft a 'One-Stop' pitch email to [Supervisor Name] regarding [Show/Project Name].
> 
> - **Reference:** Mention their recent work on [Specific Scene/Project].
>     
> - **The Hook:** Connect the 'NYC Soundscape' vibe of Steve’s music to their project.
>     
> - **The Link:** Insert the placeholder for the [Sync Bundle Link].
>     
> - **Constraint:** Keep the total word count under 120 words."
>     

## 5. Skills Gap & Training

To maximize Antigravity/Claude, ensure they are "fed" the following context from your vault:

- **The "Hero" List:** The top 10-15 tracks most likely to sync.
    
- **The Rejection Log:** If a supervisor says "too busy," the AI should update the CRM to "Follow up in 3 months."
    
- **The Asset Key:** A map of where WAVs vs. MP3s are stored (Dropbox/Drive).
