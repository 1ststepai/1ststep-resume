Frequently asked questions

How does the free account work?

You get 3 tailored resumes, 1 cover letter, a Career Positioning Brief, Role/AI Match Score, and vault preview every month at no cost. No credit card required.

What is an AI tailor?

Each tailor is one resume rewrite. You paste in your base resume and a job description, and the AI rewrites your resume to match that specific role by closing keyword gaps, reordering priorities, and reframing your experience for the role.

What does Job Hunt Pass unlock?

Job Hunt Pass is $24.99/month and unlocks unlimited resume tailoring, unlimited cover letters, the application tracker, full resume vault, Career Positioning Brief, and Chrome extension workflow.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your account settings or contact support at any time. You keep access until the end of your billing period and won't be charged again.

Is my resume data safe?

Your resume content is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate the tailored output, then returned to your browser. We don't store your resume on our servers and never use it to train AI models. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Do you store my credit card?

No. Payment is handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card number.

What if I hit my free limit?

You'll see your usage meter in the app. When you reach a free limit, you can upgrade to Job Hunt Pass at any time or wait for the next month's reset.
