Frequently asked questions

How does the free tier work?

You get 3 AI-tailored resumes and 3 cover letters every month at no cost — no credit card required. It resets on the first of each month. If you're applying to a handful of jobs it may be all you need.

What is an "AI tailor"?

Each tailor is one resume rewrite. You paste in your base resume and a job description, and the AI rewrites your resume to match that specific role — closing keyword gaps, reordering priorities, and reframing your experience to beat ATS filters. A cover letter counts separately.

What's the difference between monthly and annual billing?

Annual billing locks in a 40% discount — Essential drops from $49/mo to $29/mo (billed $348/year) and Complete drops from $99/mo to $59/mo (billed $708/year). You can cancel at any time; you'll keep access until the end of the period you paid for.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your account settings at any time. You keep access until the end of your billing period and won't be charged again.

Is my resume data safe?

Your resume content is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate the tailored output, then returned to your browser. We don't store your resume on our servers and never use it to train AI models. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Do you store my credit card?

No. Payment is handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card number.

What if I hit my monthly limit?

You'll see your usage meter in the app. When you reach your limit you can upgrade to a higher plan at any time, or wait for the next month's reset.
