## Task 1 — Morning (9am daily)

**taskId:** twitter-1ststep-morning  
**cronExpression:** 0 9 * * *  
**description:** Morning X/Twitter post for @1ststepai — job search tip or ATS insight

**Prompt angle:** ATS reality check, resume mistake, skill gap insight, or quick tip.

Post format: single tweet or 2-3 tweet thread. Hook first. Soft CTA to beta at app.1ststep.ai. Always mention it's free beta when natural. 2-3 hashtags max (#jobsearch #resume #ATS #careertips). Navigate to x.com, check logged in as @1ststepai, compose and post. Save draft to twitter_drafts/ if anything fails.

## Task 2 — Midday (12pm daily)

**taskId:** twitter-1ststep-midday  
**cronExpression:** 0 12 * * *  
**description:** Midday X/Twitter post for @1ststepai — beta spotlight or social proof angle

## Task 3 — Evening (6pm daily)

**taskId:** twitter-1ststep-evening  
**cronExpression:** 0 18 * * *  
**description:** Evening X/Twitter post for @1ststepai — story-driven or engagement post

**Prompt angle:** Job search story ("here's what a real candidate did wrong"), question to the audience ("what's the most frustrating part of applying?"), or job search math/stats angle.

Post format: more conversational, slightly longer. Ask a question at the end to drive replies. Soft CTA. This slot is for engagement, not just reach.
